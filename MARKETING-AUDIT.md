## Executive Summary

However, the site currently scores 34/100, placing it in the critical tier where fundamental marketing infrastructure issues prevent the business from being found, trusted, or converted.

Google either cannot index any content beyond the homepage shell, or is indexing it with significant delay and low confidence. The second major gap is authority and social proof. For a B2B agency selling AI systems — a high-trust, high-ticket category — the absence of third-party validation is a conversion killer.

The top three actions that would move the needle immediately: (1) migrate the site to server-side rendering or add pre-rendering, (2) create and verify a Clutch.co and Google Business Profile listing, (3) add a case study or results-focused landing page with real numbers. Implementing all recommendations could unlock an estimated **$8,000–$22,000/month** in additional pipeline value.
