## Executive Summary

Most founders and small teams lose hours a week to two unglamorous tasks: clearing email and checking what competitors are doing. This retainer automates both with AI agents that draft (never auto-send) and monitor (never overstep access boundaries) — turning two skills already built in this repo into a recurring, sellable service instead of internal tooling.

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Inbox response time cut from hours to minutes (client only approves, doesn't write)
- Weekly competitive intelligence digest instead of ad-hoc, manual checks
- Zero unsupervised sends or actions — every output is a draft or a report

---

## What's Included

### 1. Managed Inbox (powered by `agentic-inbox`)
- Daily triage of unread threads, summarized into a 2-minute morning brief
- AI-drafted replies for routine threads, queued in Gmail for one-click approval
- Proposed calendar holds for scheduling requests, confirmed before they're created
- Optional labeling/triage scheme to keep history searchable

### 2. Competitive Intelligence Feed (powered by `camofox`)
- Scheduled scrapes of up to 5 competitor sites (pricing pages, landing pages, job boards)
- Stealth-browser scraping so monitoring isn't blocked by bot detection
- Weekly digest: what changed (pricing, messaging, new pages) since last check
- Scoped to publicly accessible pages only — no login-gated or access-controlled content

### 3. (Add-on) Social Motion Pack (powered by `hyperframes`)
- 2-4 animated social clips/title cards per week, agent-authored HTML/CSS, rendered to MP4
- Best paired with an existing content calendar (e.g. this repo's `market-social` skill)
