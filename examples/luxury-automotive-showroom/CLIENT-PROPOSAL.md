## 3. Strategy & Approach

Our approach has three phases:

**Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1-2):** Brand and asset audit, content/media plan, copy and headline direction (A/B headline options included), wireframe approval.

**Phase 2 — Build (Weeks 3-5):** Full build of the showroom experience — 3D hero stage, scroll-driven spec section, inventory showcase, interior/lifestyle media, social proof, and a qualified-lead capture flow. Mobile-optimized fallback for all motion/3D elements.

**Phase 3 — Launch & Handoff (Week 6):** QA across devices, performance pass (Lighthouse/load time), deployment, and a walkthrough so your team can update inventory copy and swap media without calling us for every change.
