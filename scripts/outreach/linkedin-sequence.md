# LinkedIn DM Sequence — Ad Audit Offer
# Target: Marketing directors, business owners, CMOs actively running paid ads
# Limit: 50 connection requests/day, 50 DMs/day (stay under LinkedIn limits)
# Cadence: Request → Day 0 (accept) → Day 2 → Day 5 → Day 9

---

## STEP 1 — Connection Request Note (140 chars max)

**Version A (direct):**
> Hey {{first_name}} — saw you're running ads for {{company_name}}. Have something specific to share. Worth connecting?

**Version B (curiosity):**
> {{first_name}} — noticed something in your ad account from the outside. Wanted to flag it before connecting.

**Version C (compliment + hook):**
> Been following {{company_name}} for a while. Running ads in {{industry}} is brutal right now. Would love to connect.

---

## MESSAGE 1 — After They Accept (Day 0, send within 1 hour)

Hey {{first_name}}, thanks for connecting.

Quick context on why I reached out — I audit paid ad accounts for {{industry}} businesses and noticed {{company_name}} is running {{platform}} ads.

Not pitching anything yet. Just curious: are you happy with your current ROAS, or is there room you feel like you're leaving on the table?

---

## MESSAGE 2 — Follow-Up (Day 2, if no reply)

Hey {{first_name}} —

Didn't want this to get buried.

I did a quick external look at {{company_name}}'s ads from what's publicly visible (Meta Ad Library, Google Transparency Center).

One thing stood out: {{specific_observation}}. (Example: "Your creative has been running unchanged for ~10 weeks — usually a sign of fatigue building up.")

Happy to share what a full diagnostic would look like if it's useful. No obligation.

Worth 15 minutes to see if that's the case for {{company_name}}?

## Specific Observations by Situation (for Message 2)

Use one of these based on what you find in the ad library:

| What You See | What to Write |
|---|---|
| Same creative running 8+ weeks | "Your creative hasn't rotated in about 2 months — that's usually when CPM starts climbing even with a healthy offer." |
| No retargeting ads visible | "I don't see any retargeting creative in your Meta library — you might be paying for cold traffic and not recapturing it." |
| Very few ad variations | "You're running fewer than 5 creative variants on Meta — Andromeda's algorithm rewards diversity; limited variation usually means overpaying." |
| High ad frequency estimated | "Based on your library, looks like you might be hitting frequency saturation with your current audience." |
| No video ads | "All your Meta ads appear to be static — video typically drives 30–50% lower CPMs right now." |
| Running broad match without Smart Bidding signals | "Broad match without strong Smart Bidding signals is one of the fastest ways to hemorrhage Google budget." |

## LinkedIn Outreach Rules

- Profile must look legit: professional photo, clear headline ("I help [niche] businesses fix leaking ad accounts"), recent activity/posts
- Do NOT pitch in the connection request
- Do NOT send a wall of text — short messages win on LinkedIn
- Personalize Message 1 every time — generic opener gets ignored
- Stop at Message 4 — more than that damages your SSI score
- Use Sales Navigator for filtering: title = Owner/CMO/Marketing Director, company size = 11–200, industry = target niche
