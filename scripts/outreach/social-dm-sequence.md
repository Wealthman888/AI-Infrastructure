# Instagram & Facebook DM Sequence — Ad Audit Offer
# Target: Business owners and marketers who post about their business / run visible ads
# Volume: 50 DMs/day per platform
# Cadence: Day 1 → Day 3 → Day 6

---

## INSTAGRAM SEQUENCE

### IG Message 1 — Opener (Day 1)

**Version A — Observation hook:**
> Hey {{first_name}} — just saw your ad for {{product/service}} in my feed.
> Quick question: are you happy with what it's converting at, or is there room to push it further?

**Version B — Compliment + question:**
> Been following {{account_name}} for a bit — love what you're building.
> Are you running paid ads to grow it or mostly organic right now?

**Version C — Specific creative note:**
> Hey — noticed your ad has been running the same visual for a while. On Meta that usually means the algorithm starts charging more per click around week 6–8. Has your cost per result been climbing lately?

---

### IG Message 2 — Value drop (Day 3, if no reply)

> {{first_name}} — didn't want to ghost after that first message.
>
> I audit ad accounts for businesses like yours. Most find $5k–$20k in fixable issues in the first pass — wasted spend, tracking gaps, creative fatigue, wrong bid strategy.
>
> If you want I can run a quick look at your account and tell you exactly what's leaking. No cost, just a 15-min call to walk through it.
>
> Worth it?

---

### IG Message 3 — Final (Day 6)

> Last one from me {{first_name}} — don't want to be that person in your DMs.
>
> If you ever feel like your ad spend isn't working as hard as it should, here's how to reach me: {{calendar_link or email}}
>
> Good luck with everything — your content is genuinely great.

## FACEBOOK SEQUENCE

### FB Message 1 — Opener (Day 1)

**Version A — Group/post context:**
> Hey {{first_name}} — saw your post in {{group_name}} about {{topic}}.
> Quick question: what platform are you running ads on right now — Meta, Google, or both?

**Version B — Business page context:**
> Hey {{first_name}} — came across {{business_name}}'s page.
> Are you running paid ads to bring in new clients or still mostly referrals?

**Version C — Ad spotted:**
> Hey — your ad for {{offer}} just showed up in my feed.
> Curious — what's it converting at right now? I work with a lot of {{industry}} businesses and can usually spot what's holding the numbers back.

---

## Targeting — Who to DM

### Instagram
- Business owners with 1k–100k followers posting about their product/service
- People running visible ads (check their ad library at facebook.com/ads/library)
- Accounts boosting posts (visible from "Sponsored" tag)
- Followers of industry accounts (competitors, trade publications)
- Hashtag mining: #{{industry}}ads, #{{industry}}marketing, #paidads, #googleads, #facebookads

### Facebook
- Members of business/marketing Facebook Groups who post questions about ads
- Business page owners in your target niche
- People asking "why are my ads not working" in entrepreneur groups
- Local business owners in city-specific business groups

## DM Rules (Platform Safety)

| Platform | Daily Limit | Safe Limit | Warm-Up |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram | ~100 DMs | 30–50/day | Start at 10/day for first 2 weeks |
| Facebook | ~100 DMs | 30–50/day | Use aged personal account, not new |

- Never send identical messages back to back — vary openers
- Do NOT include links in Message 1 (triggers spam filters)
- React to / comment on a post before DMing when possible (warms the conversation)
- If account is < 6 months old, keep volume at 20/day until trust score builds
- Space DMs 2–5 minutes apart minimum — batch sending = shadow ban risk
