# Qualification Funnel — Ad Audit & Management
# Purpose: Pre-qualify leads so you only take calls with serious buyers
# Flow: Outreach → VSL Page → Application Form → Pre-Call Email → Discovery Call → Close

---

## FUNNEL OVERVIEW

```
Cold Outreach (Email / LinkedIn / IG / FB)
         ↓
    VSL Landing Page
  (2-min video + apply button)
         ↓
   Application Form
  (5 qualifying questions)
         ↓
  Auto-Confirm Email
  (sets expectations, builds authority)
         ↓
   Discovery Call
  (15–20 min, diagnose + pitch)
         ↓
    Close / Audit Delivery
```

## VSL SCRIPT (2 minutes, selfie-style video)

**[0–15 sec] HOOK**
> "If you're spending money on ads and you're not 100% sure where every dollar is going — this 2-minute video will show you exactly what's happening and what to do about it."

**[15–40 sec] PROBLEM**
> "Most business owners running paid ads are losing 20–40% of their budget to things that are completely fixable. Bad tracking. Creative fatigue. Wasted spend on the wrong keywords. The problem is it's nearly impossible to see from inside the account — you need someone who looks at dozens of accounts to spot the patterns."

**[40–75 sec] CREDIBILITY + PROOF**
> "I've audited over [X] ad accounts in the last [X] months. The average account I look at has [3–5] critical issues that are quietly killing performance. One client was spending $18k/month and their ROAS looked fine on the surface — we found they were losing $6k/month to irrelevant traffic and another $4k to a broken conversion event. Fixed both in a week."

**[75–100 sec] THE OFFER**
> "Here's what I do: I run a full 48-hour audit across your Google and Meta accounts — 250+ checks covering tracking, creative, budget, keywords, and landing pages. You get a prioritized report that tells you exactly what's wrong and how to fix it, in order of revenue impact."

**[100–115 sec] CTA**
> "If you're spending at least $5,000 a month on ads and you want to know if your account is healthy — hit the button below, fill out the short form, and I'll get back to you within 24 hours to set up a free 15-minute diagnostic call."

## DISCOVERY CALL FRAMEWORK (15–20 min)

### Structure

**[0–2 min] Frame the call**
> "Here's how I want to use our time: I'm going to ask you a few questions about your setup, share what I'm seeing from the outside, and then we'll both figure out if the audit makes sense. Sound good?"

**[2–8 min] Diagnose**
Ask:
1. "Walk me through your current setup — which platforms, what's your monthly spend, and what's your main conversion goal?"
2. "What's your ROAS / CPA target, and where are you actually landing?"
3. "How long has it been at that level? Was there a point when it was better?"
4. "Have you made any major changes recently — new creative, new campaigns, budget changes?"
5. "How confident are you in your conversion tracking right now?"

**[8–13 min] Show the problem**
Pull up what you can see externally (Meta Ad Library, Google Transparency Center) and name 2–3 specific issues. Make it real.

> "Based on what you just told me and what I can see from the outside, here's what I think is happening…"

**[13–17 min] Pitch the audit**
> "The full audit takes 48 hours. I go through 250+ checkpoints across [their platforms], give you a 0–100 health score, and a prioritized fix list — Critical, High, Medium, Low — with exactly what to do for each one. Most clients recover the audit fee in the first 7 days of fixes."
>
> "The investment is ${{audit_price}}. Want to move forward today?"

**[17–20 min] Handle objections + close**

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I need to think about it" | "What specifically do you need to think through? I want to make sure you have everything you need to decide." |
| "It's too expensive" | "Compared to what you're spending on ads each month, we're talking about less than one day's ad spend to find out if the rest is being wasted." |
| "Can you just tell me what's wrong now?" | "I can tell you what I suspect from the outside, but the actual issues are inside the account — that's what the audit is for. What I showed you today is maybe 20% of what I find once I'm in." |
| "We already have someone managing our ads" | "Even better — the audit gives your team a prioritized roadmap. Most agencies love having an outside diagnostic because it validates what they've been saying or surfaces things they missed." |

## DISQUALIFICATION RULES

**Do not take the call if:**
- Monthly ad spend < $2,000 (not enough to audit meaningfully — redirect to a self-serve resource)
- They only want "a few tips" for free (they won't buy)
- They filled out the form with one-word answers (low intent)
- They say "I just want to know if we should even be running ads" (not the right fit — wrong stage)

**Auto-disqualify response:**
> "Based on your answers, I don't think the full audit is the right next step for you — it's built for accounts with more data to work with. Here's what I'd suggest instead: [resource or alternative offer]. If you scale to $5k/month in ad spend, come back and I'd love to help."
