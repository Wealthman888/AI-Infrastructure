# Axiom — Outreach Kit (12-Day Sprint)

Goal: 3 closed clients in 12 days. Motion: build a free personalized demo of their CURRENT listing, then convert to paid.

---

## Email Sequence (3 touches)

### Email 1 — Day 1 (the demo reveal)
**Subject:** I rebuilt your [Property Street] listing — take a look
**Preview text:** No charge, just wanted to show you this.

Body:
```
Hi [Agent First Name],

I came across your listing at [Property Address] and built a quick
cinematic version of it — AI-generated hero film, gallery stills, and
a branded one-page site, the way buyers would experience it if they
clicked from Instagram instead of the MLS.

Here it is: [demo link]

No ask here — just wanted you to see it. If it's not useful, no worries.
If you want the real thing built for your live listings, happy to talk.

[Your Name]
```
CTA: view the link. Goal: open rate + click, not reply.

### Email 2 — Day 4 (case study + soft ask)
**Subject:** Re: your [Property Street] listing
**Preview text:** Here's what this did for another Malibu listing.

Body:
```
Hi [Agent First Name],

Following up on the demo I sent for [Property Address] — here's a real
example: Casa Lumière, a $6.95M Malibu estate, with the same treatment:
[Casa Lumière link]

Listings like this tend to get more saves and shares because the buyer
feels the property before they ever schedule a tour.

Want me to build the full version for [Property Address] this week?
Flat rate, $1,500–2,500, live in 48 hours.

[Your Name]
```
CTA: "Want me to build the full version?" Goal: reply/booked call.

### Email 3 — Day 8 (breakup + door open)
**Subject:** Closing the loop
**Preview text:** No pressure — just leaving this here.

Body:
```
Hi [Agent First Name],

Don't want to clutter your inbox — this is my last note on this.
The demo's still live here if you want it: [demo link]

If timing's better down the road, happy to revisit. Either way, wishing
you a fast close on [Property Address].

[Your Name]
```
CTA: leave door open. Goal: low-pressure close, salvage referral.

## DM Script (Instagram / LinkedIn)

Use when the agent posts a new listing — comment isn't enough, go to DM same day.

### DM 1 (Day 0, same day as their listing post)
```
Hey [First Name] — saw your new listing at [Property Address], it's
stunning. I build cinematic AI listing sites (hero film + branded page)
for agents like you — put together a free demo of yours, want me to
send it over?
```
Keep it under 3 sentences. No link in the first message — earns the reply, avoids spam filters/ignored unsolicited links.

### DM 2 (after they reply "sure"/"yes")
```
Here it is: [demo link]
Took about a day to build, no charge — just wanted you to see what it
could look like. If you like it, I can do the real version for your
live listings, $1,500–2,500 flat, 48hr turnaround.
```

### DM 3 (if no reply after 3 days, single bump — no more)
```
No pressure if it's not a fit right now — the demo's still up here if
you want to check it later: [demo link]
```

---

## Usage Notes
- Personalize [Property Address] / [Agent First Name] every time — generic = ignored.
- Lead with the free demo, not the pitch. The ask comes in touch 2, never touch 1.
- Cap at 3 touches per channel. If no response, move on — don't chase past the breakup email/DM.
- Track in a simple sheet: Agent | Listing | Channel | Demo sent date | Opened? | Replied? | Status.
- Target volume: 10-15 new prospects/day during Days 5-7 to get enough surface area for 3 closes.
